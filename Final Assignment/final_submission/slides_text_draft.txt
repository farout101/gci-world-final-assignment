SLIDE 4: PROBLEM DEFINITION & TARGET
-------------------------------------
Message: Predict "silent churn" (usage compression) 31-60 days before a subscriber abandons their SIM.
Key Points:
- **Target variable**: `churn` (1 = subscriber shifted traffic or became inactive 31-60 days post-observation).
- Prepaid subscribers rarely cancel formally; they simply stop reloading their SIM cards.
- **Goal**: Predict disengagement to trigger wallet promotions (KBZPay/Wave Money) *before* they switch networks.
